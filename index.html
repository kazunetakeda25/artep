In the Heart of KL’s Heritage

The adventurous, curious traveller and the creative community now has a place to visit, to meet and to chill. A hotel at the Heart of Kuala Lumpur’s new creative hub.

Kuala Lumpur is known as a city of change, constantly transforming and renewing itself. Kampung Attap, where Artép Hotel is located will be Kuala Lumpur’s next creative centre providing hotel and residential facilities. Its unique location is close proximity to landmarks of historical places. The soul of the city, around ChinaTown, Masjid Jamek and Merdeka Square, where the vintage city centre and the River of Life are located just minutes away.
